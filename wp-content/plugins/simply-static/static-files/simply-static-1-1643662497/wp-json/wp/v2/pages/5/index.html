Abstract<\/h2>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t

Industry 4.0 refers to a collection of novel technologies and methods that involve intelligent and smart products and production processes creating a manufacturing environment in which all participants are interconnected and share information with each other. The main purpose of this research project is to explore (a) the specific challenges of implementing Industry 4.0 solutions at manufacturing companies located in Central-Eastern Europe, including the best practices that can be used to overcome these challenges, and (b) the performance impact of successfully implemented Industry 4.0 solutions at these manufacturing companies. Given the novelty of this research area, primarily exploratory methods are applied by which we aim to carry out empirical case study research at manufacturing companies located in Central-Eastern Europe. To increase the generalizability of our results, a follow-up survey study is also planned, covering at least 5 Central-Eastern European countries, to extend the validity of findings.<\/p>\n

Expected results aim to bring significant theoretical contribution to the field, as current literature offers only limited knowledge on the implementation challenges of Industry 4.0 projects, on best practices used to overcome these challenges, as well as on the actual performance outcomes of implementing Industry 4.0 solutions. Furthermore, current literature offers no evidence whether initial results related to Industry 4.0 projects at developed country manufacturing companies are also valid for companies located in the Central-Eastern European region.<\/p>\n

From a practical perspective we aim to offer a powerful roadmap for the managers of local manufacturing companies to identify and overcome the challenges of implementing new technologies related to the Industry 4.0 concept, as well as a benchmark on the performance impact of successfully implemented Industry 4.0 solutions, thereby aiming to increase the competitiveness of these companies.<\/p><\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/section>\n\t\t\t\t
